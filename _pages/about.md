I am a Ph.D. student in the [School of EECS](https://www.kth.se/eecs) at [KTH Royal Institute of Technology](https://www.kth.se/en), supervised by [Prof. Dejan Kostić](https://dejankosticgithub.github.io/index.html). My research focuses on efficient large language model systems and multi-agent systems.

Outside academia, I am an AI Engineer Intern at [Eigent AI](https://www.eigent.ai/), contributing to large-scale LLM agent frameworks including [CAMEL](https://www.camel-ai.org/), and a Founding Engineer at [OrbDB Labs](https://orbdb.github.io/), building a hybrid database engine that integrates Graph ML inference into query execution.

Previously, I completed my Master's in Machine Learning at KTH, with an exchange semester at [EPFL](https://www.epfl.ch/en/), and my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Technology at [Harbin Institute of Technology](https://www.hit.edu.cn/), where I worked as a research assistant supervised by [Prof. Hongzhi Wang](http://homepage.hit.edu.cn/wang) and [Prof. Zhizheng Wu](https://drwuz.com/) at [CUHK-Shenzhen](https://www.cuhk.edu.cn/en).
