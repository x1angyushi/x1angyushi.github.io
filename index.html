Xiangyu Shi

Ph.D. Student at KTH Royal Institute of Technology

I am a Ph.D. student in the School of EECS at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, supervised by Prof. Dejan Kostić. My research focuses on efficient large language model systems and multi-agent systems.

Outside academia, I am an AI Engineer Intern at Eigent AI, contributing to large-scale LLM agent frameworks including CAMEL, and a Founding Engineer at OrbDB Labs, building a hybrid database engine that integrates Graph ML inference into query execution.

Previously, I completed my Master’s in Machine Learning at KTH, with an exchange semester at EPFL, and my Bachelor’s in Computer Science and Technology at Harbin Institute of Technology, where I worked as a research assistant supervised by Prof. Hongzhi Wang and Prof. Zhizheng Wu at CUHK-Shenzhen.
